<p><strong>Key Programmes of Department</strong>
</p>
<p>In Anderson, we have an integrated framework that provides a dynamic Science
learning environment for students with the aim of fostering critical and
inventive thinking as well as developing communication skills in our students.</p>
<p>1) Science Research Programme (SRP)</p>
<p>Our SRP provides a stimulating learning environment beyond the traditional
curriculum, fostering a passion for scientific discovery and research.</p>
<p>We equip students with the necessary skills to become independent researchers,
fostering critical thinking, problem-solving as well as inspiring innovative
approaches to scientific challenges.</p>
<p></p>

<p>2) Science Talent Programme (STP)</p>
<p>Our STP offers a dynamic and enriching learning experience:</p>
<p><strong>Olympiad Training:</strong> Dedicated training sessions for challenging
competitions like the Singapore Junior Chemistry Olympiad and the Singapore
Junior Physics Olympiad.</p>
<p><strong>External Science Competitions:</strong> Opportunities to participate
in prestigious competitions like the C.B. Paul Science Quiz, the Biomedical
Quiz, and the National STEM Challenge, allowing students to showcase their
talents.</p>
