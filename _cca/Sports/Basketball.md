<p>Basketball@ANDSS is where passion meets skill and dreams take flight on

<p>We believe in the transformative power of sports. Through structured training
sessions, friendly competitions, and engaging drills, our students not

lessons such as discipline, perseverance, and leadership skills.</p>
<p>Led by experienced coaches who are passionate about both the sport and
the holistic development of our students, our programme caters to players

a place for you here in our CCA.</p>
<p>Beyond the court, our CCA fosters a sense of camaraderie and belonging
among its members. Through team-building activities and social events,
students forge lasting friendships and develop essential social skills
that extend far beyond the game.</p>
