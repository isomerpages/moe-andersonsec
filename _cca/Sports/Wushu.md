<p><strong>WUSHU</strong>
</p>
<p>Anderson Wushu emerges as a highly competitive Co-Curricular Activity
(CCA), as evidenced by the remarkable achievements of our members at the
2023 National School Games. With dedicated training sessions, our athletes
are primed for success in both competitions and performances. We also focus
on enabling our athletes to contribute to the community, and this is seen
through several performances in school, and their involvement in the Mother
Tongue fortnight programmes.</p>
<p>Beyond the pursuit of medals in competitions, our focus extends to nurturing
holistic development in our students. We instil values of healthy living
and exercise, professionalism, commitment, and ethical conduct, and consistently
guide our athletes towards a meaningful life both on and off the Wushu
carpet. Coupled together with the school values, we aspire for all our
athletes to exemplify dedication, skill, and values-driven leadership in
all aspects of life.</p>
