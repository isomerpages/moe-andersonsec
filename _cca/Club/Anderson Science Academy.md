<p><strong>Science Academy</strong>
</p>
<p>The Science Academy is more than just a club; it is a community of aspiring
scientists, engineers, and innovators. Students can hone their scientific
inquiry skills through engaging activities, mentorship programmes and research
projects.</p>
<p><strong>Vision:</strong>
</p>
<p>To nurture the next generation of scientific innovators in Singapore by
igniting a passion for scientific inquiry and research in students throughout
their secondary school education.</p>
<p><strong>Mission:</strong>
</p>
<p>• To nurture a curious and inquisitive spirit in students through hands-on
science exploration.</p>
<p>• To provide a platform for students to develop critical thinking, problem-solving,
and research skills.</p>
<p>• To equip students with the knowledge and confidence to participate in
science fairs and competitions.</p>
<p>• To cultivate a collaborative learning environment where students can
share ideas and learn from one another.</p>
<p><strong>Programmes and Activities:</strong>
</p>
<p>We offer a variety of engaging activities for our students to</p>
<p>• deepen the understanding of scientific concepts beyond the classroom
curriculum,</p>
<p>• gain hands-on experience through experiments and projects,</p>
<p>• embark on exciting scientific research projects guided by experienced
mentors,</p>
<p>• participate in science fairs and competitions to showcase students’
research and innovation projects,</p>
<p>• interact with guest speakers from various scientific fields, and</p>
<p>• develop valuable research, communication, and teamwork skills.</p>
